Our compromise

Mate Tostado Co. has made a commitment to sustainable farming practices. We implements several key strategies to ensure that our farming practices are as sustainable as possible.

  • First, we use organic farming methods to grow yerba mate. This means that we do not use any synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, which can have a negative impact on the environment and the health of local communities.
  • In addition, we promote our renowned agroforestry system, which involves growing yerba mate alongside other native trees and plants. This helps to preserve biodiversity and support the natural ecosystem.

    • Overall, Mate Tostado is committed to sustainable farming practices that are good for the environment, local communities, and the quality of our final product. By choosing to purchase yerba mate from us, consumers can feel good knowing that they are supporting a brand that is making a positive impact on the world.
