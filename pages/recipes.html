How to enjoy a robust and flavorful infusion

Mate Tostado is a traditional South American beverage made from the leaves of Yerba Mate plant. The leaves are harvested, dried, and then roasted to give them a unique, smoky flavor.

  • Our product has a distinct, smoky flavor that is balanced by a slight sweetness and a hint of bitterness. It is a rich source of caffeine, antioxidants and minerals, which makes it a great alternative to coffee or tea. It's also enjoyed for its energizing and refreshing properties.
  • This beverage can be enjoyed either hot or cold. When consumed cold, it is traditionally drunk from a hollowed-out gourd called a "mate" and sipped through a metal straw called a "bombilla." It is often mixed with cold water or lemon juice and served over ice, this version is called "tereré".

Instructions

  • 4 spoons of Mate Tostado
  • 1 liter of boiling water
  • Mix them, and let it rest for around 5 minutes
  • Sweeten it with honey, sugar or any artificial sweetener
  • Add lemon juice, orange juice or lime to the mixture
  • Don't forget to add some mint and ice if you want it cold and refreshing!
